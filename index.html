FAQ

What drinks and food served on board the aircraft?

We offer premium catering options including gourmet meals, fine wines, champagne, and specialty beverages. All dietary requirements and preferences can be accommodated with advance notice.

How much time will I save on a charter flight?

Charter flights typically save 2-4 hours compared to commercial flights due to reduced check-in times, no layovers, access to smaller airports, and flexible scheduling.

Can I cancel my flight?

Yes, flights can be cancelled according to our flexible cancellation policy. Terms vary depending on the notice period and specific charter agreement.
